DUGOGODIŠNJA TRADICIJA A LONG-TERM TRADITION

ALMINA DOO je osnovana 1988. godine kao zanatska radnja. Na samom početku, bavili smo se mašinskom obradom struganjem i brušenjem i izradom mašina, alata i mašinskih delova. Nakon dve godine, proizvodni program je proširen konstruisanjem i izradom mašine za profilisanje i izradu otvorenih, hladno valjanih profila različite namene, dimenzija i oblika. Iste godine je puštena u rad i linija za izradu montažnih , metalnih polica široke namene, a iz pogona za brizganje plastike, izašlo je i grlo za fluo rasvetu. Tokom devedesetih godina (i pored teških uslova za poslovanje) svi postojeći proizvodni programi su obogaćeni novim artiklima. Usvojili smo kompletnu proizvodnju nosača kablova i gromobranske opreme. U cilju zaokruživanja proizvodnje, 2002. godine pustili smo u rad liniju za elektro statičko farbanja-plastificiranje metala. ALMINA DOO was founded in 1988 as a craft shop. At the very beginning, we were engaged in turning and grinding machining and making machines, tools and machine parts. After two years, the production program was expanded by designing and manufacturing machines for profiling and manufacturing open, cold-rolled profiles of various purposes, dimensions and shapes. In the same year, a line for the production of prefab, metal shelves for general purposes was put into operation, and from the plant for injection molding, a socket for fluo lighting came out. During the nineties (despite the difficult business conditions), all existing production programs were enriched with new items. We have adopted the complete production of cable carriers and lightning protection equipment. In order to round off the production, in 2002 we put into operation a line for electro static painting-plastification of metals.

U godinama za nama trudili smo se da podignemo proizvodne mogućnosti i kvalitet samih proizvoda. Danas u našem proizvodnom pogonu imamo CNC i NC mašine za savijanje i sečenje limova do 3000mm, mašine i alate za probijanje i izvlačenje limova. Varenje delova i sklopova se radi na kvalitetnoj opremi uz obučeni kadar, tako da osim čelika možemo pružiti usluge varenja aluminijuma i inoxa. Antikorozivna zaštita se vrši elektrostatičkim farbanjem-plastificirsnjem na najsavremenijim uređajima uz korišćenje visoko kvalitetnih boja, kao i odmašćivača za pripremu farbanja. Komora za pečenje-polimerizaciju je dužine 6000mm. In the years that followed, we tried to increase production capabilities and the quality of the products themselves. Today in our production plant we have CNC and NC machines for bending and cutting sheets up to 3000mm, machines and tools for punching and drawing sheets. Welding of parts and assemblies is done on high-quality equipment with trained staff, so in addition to steel, we can provide aluminum and stainless steel welding services. Anti-corrosion protection is carried out by electrostatic painting-plasticizing on the most modern devices with the use of high-quality paints, as well as degreasers for the preparation of painting. The firing-polymerization chamber is 6000mm long.

U 2020. godini, mašinski park je obogaćen mašinama za brizganje plastike, a tada je usvojen i program izrade distancera i dodatne opreme za termo izolovane cevi. In 2020, the machinery park was enriched with plastic injection machines, and then the program for the production of spacers and additional equipment for thermally insulated pipes was adopted.
